Sidebar Code is the consulting and product arm of Banfield Consulting, LLC, founded by Kyle Banfield. As a practicing attorney at CHDB Law, LLP, Kyle builds the AI workflows he uses every day, then packages them for other firms.

He understands how to take a deposition, how insurance carriers evaluate billing, how a partner should review associate work product, and how to draft a motion under time pressure.
